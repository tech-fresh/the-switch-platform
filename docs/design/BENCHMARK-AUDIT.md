# The Switch Platform — Benchmark Audit

Use these products for design inspiration without copying them.

## Seneca

Use: learning flow, quiz rhythm, structured revision.  
Avoid: dense menus or visual overload.

## Duolingo

Use: motivation, streaks, simple progress.  
Avoid: over-gamification.

## Linear

Use: premium navigation, calm layout, precise spacing.  
Avoid: making the app feel too technical.

## Stripe

Use: polished sections, strong spacing, professional cards.  
Avoid: marketing complexity inside student pages.

## Apple

Use: typography, whitespace, simplicity.  
Avoid: hiding core learning tools.

## Notion

Use: organisation and information hierarchy.  
Avoid: too much nesting.

## BBC Bitesize

Use: educational clarity and accessible language.  
Avoid: dated visual style.

## Save My Exams

Use: exam content structure and topic focus.  
Avoid: text-heavy pages.

## Rule

The Switch Platform should be premium, vibrant, accessible, and original.
