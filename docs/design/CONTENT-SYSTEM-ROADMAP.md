# Content System Roadmap

## Purpose

This document summarises the long-term content architecture for The Switch Platform and complements MVP Priority #8 (Content Editorial).

## Guiding Principles
- Official exam-board specifications are the source of truth.
- No copyrighted revision content is copied.
- AI assists content creation but never bypasses QA.
- No AI-generated student content is published without editorial approval.
- All content is specification-driven and modular.
- Annual specification updates should be data updates, not code rewrites.

## Planned Phases
1. Content Governance
2. Specification Engine
3. Subject Library
4. Revision Content Engine
5. Question Engine
6. Variant Engine
7. Answer & Mark Scheme Engine
8. Content QA Framework
9. Editorial CMS
10. Assessment Generator
11. Personalisation
12. Analytics
13. Future AI Tutor (architecture only)

## MVP Subject Library
- GCSE Mathematics
- GCSE English Language
- GCSE Combined Science
- iGCSE Mathematics

Science architecture must distinguish Combined Science from Separate (Triple) Science while allowing shared content.

## Content QA
Every item must pass:
- Specification validation
- Answer validation
- Mark scheme validation
- Difficulty validation
- Accessibility validation
- Duplicate detection
- Confidence scoring

Only approved content may be published.

## Long-term Architecture
Questions, revision notes, flashcards, worked examples, assessments, recommendations and progress all link to Learning Objectives within the Specification Engine.
