# Motivation Guide

The daily motivation card is part of the MVP dashboard.

## Purpose

Give learners one positive, age-appropriate message each day without adding clutter.

## Rules

- Use reviewed content only.
- Use reputable sources.
- Keep content suitable for ages 11 to 18.
- Keep wording positive and inclusive.
- Keep it appropriate for school use.
- Do not use live internet feeds in the MVP.

## Representation

The library should include people from Africa, Europe, Asia, North America, South America, and Oceania.

Include historical figures and modern role models current as of 2026 and beyond.

## Metadata

Store text, person, country or region, contribution, theme, source, status, and review date.
